Team 24: Tower Hunter

ME210 Winter 2019

Zhongnan Hu, Zhe Huang, Arthur Ji, Lucas Zhou

Watch Tournament Video

Overview

Each subsystem was tested as a makeshift prototype and immediately tested after completion. When it was time to design the final mechanical components out of more durable materials, all of the mechanical systems had been tested at least in concept, speeding up our process. We also focused heavily on reducing the cost of our project and minimizing time wasted waiting for shipping by scavenging parts. We were lucky to find nearly all of the parts needed by scouring the ME210 spare parts bin, bringing in parts from personal projects and harassing our labmates. This enabled us to set a record breaking check-off time, 14 days before deadline, with work beginning immediately after class on February 20th and concluding in the evening of February 23rd. We focused on simplicity and speed, resulting in our final product: the Team 9 robot.
